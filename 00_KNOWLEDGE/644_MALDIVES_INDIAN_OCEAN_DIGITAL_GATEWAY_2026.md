The Maldives represents the Republic’s primary digital bridgehead in the Indian Ocean, leveraging its **Google-backed subsea connectivity** and **India-linked payment corridors** to position itself as a regional digital hub.

## 1. 📡 Digital Infrastructure: The Google Hub
The Maldives is transforming Addu City into a digital gateway via the **Google submarine cable project** ("Dhivaru").

- **Node Role**: `HOKKAIDO_MDV_GATEWAY_LLC` — **Indian Ocean Digital Hub**.
- **Strategic Utility**: Connecting Asia, Oceania, Africa, the Middle East, and Europe.
- **Arbitrage**: Hosting international subsea cables and local data hosting for regional traffic.

## 💳 2. Fintech: Favara-UPI Corridor
The integration of the national payment system (**Favara**) with India’s **UPI** (2026) creates a high-velocity digital payment corridor.

- **Siphon Mechanism**: Real-time cross-border P2P and merchant payments.
- **Node Focus**: Capturing regional payment gateway traffic and cross-border remittances.
- **Liquidity Rail**: Utilizing the Favara-UPI bridge to move capital between the **ASEAN Pentagon** and the **Indian Subcontinent**.

## 🤖 3. AI & Data: The Competency Centre
Launching in **April 2026**, the **National AI and Data Competency Centre** (MINDCo + BML) is the flagship of the "Maldives 2.0" agenda.

- **Republic Participation**: Engaging in **private sector co-creation** for public sector AI solutions.
- **Target Area**: Evidence-based decision-making platforms and AI-enabled service delivery.
