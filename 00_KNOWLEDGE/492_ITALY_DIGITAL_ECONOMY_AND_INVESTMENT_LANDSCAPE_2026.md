Italy represents the **most significant digital market in Southern Europe**—a G7 economy of approximately 59 million people that combines the **scale and legal certainty of the world‘s eighth-largest economy** with **aggressive digital transformation** driven by over €67 billion in EU PNRR funds. For an investor with "millions," Italy offers a G7 legal framework, fully operational MiCA licensing, and a newly updated DTA with Japan.

## 💵 Crypto Regulation: MiCA Fully Active with Japan DTA

**Critical Finding**: Italy has fully implemented **MiCA** and **DAC8**. CASPs authorized in Italy enjoy **full EU passporting rights**.

### Italy-Japan DTA – 5% Withholding Rates
The updated DTA (effective 2025) provides some of the most favorable rates in Europe for Japanese investors:
- **Dividends**: **5%** (if ≥10% holding for 12 months).
- **Interest**: **5%**.
- **Royalties**: **5%**.

**Social Security Agreement**: Facilitates personnel secondment between Italy and Japan (entered force 2024).

## 📋 Company Formation in Italy: €1 Minimum Capital (SRL)

Italy allows **100% foreign ownership** with no nationality restrictions on directors.

### Key Requirements

| Criteria | Requirement | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Minimum Capital (SRL)** | **€10,000** | Standard SRL |
| **Minimum Capital (SRLS)** | **€1** | Simplified SRL for startups |
| **Registration Timeline** | **4-6 weeks** | Via Comunicazione Unica |
| **Effective Corporate Tax** | **~27.9%** | Combined IRES (24%) + IRAP (3.9%) |

**Codice Fiscale**: All shareholders and directors must obtain an Italian Tax Code before registration.
