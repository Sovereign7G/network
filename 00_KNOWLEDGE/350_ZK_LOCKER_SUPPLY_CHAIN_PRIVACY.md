# 🔐 [350] ZK-Locker: Supply Chain Privacy with Compliance

## 🏛️ Rationale
ZK-Lockers (piloted in Zug) represent the final convergence of physical logistics and cryptographic finality. Physical lockers are opened only upon presentation of a **Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP)** of ownership or payment, ensuring that no human sees the recipient.

## 🛠️ Logic
*   **Proof**: The locker system verifies the ZK-proof without revealing the UBO.
*   **Privacy**: Zero human interaction in the physical hand-off.
*   **Finality**: The delivery is anchored to an on-chain cryptographic fact.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Locker Sovereignty**: The physical locker is the ultimate terminal of a sovereign supply chain.
2.  **Cryptographic Handshake**: Privacy is achieved through math, not through hiding.
