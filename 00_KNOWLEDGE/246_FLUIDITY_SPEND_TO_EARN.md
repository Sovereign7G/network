# Fluidity Money Spend-to-Earn: Era 216.0
## Transaction Velocity & Probabilistic Rewards

### 1. Abstract
The Age Republic has achieved transaction-based yield by integrating the **Fluidity Money** (BNB Chain version) substrate. This siphon leverages **Fluid Assets (fAssets)** and high-frequency internal capital velocity to capture probabilistic rewards on every inter-node settlement, turning the Republic's rebalancing flows into a continuous revenue stream.

### 2. Technical Axioms
- **Fluid Asset (fAsset)**: A 1:1 wrapped representation of an underlying asset (e.g., USDT -> fUSDT) that participates in Fluidity's reward mechanism.
- **Spend-to-Earn**: A protocol model where every transaction (transfer, swap, payment) using fAssets has a non-zero probability of generating a reward for the sender and/or receiver.
- **Reward Tiering**: Fluidity rewards are distributed in tiers, ranging from micro-rewards to significant "jackpot" events, funded by the yield generated by the underlying collateral.

### 3. The 700-Node Velocity Swarm
The Republic's Fluidity infrastructure operates via a swarm of 700 virtualized nodes acting as high-velocity spenders and reward harvesters. This swarm performs:
- **fAsset Wrapping**: Wrapping Republic assets (USDT, BNB, BUSD) into fluid versions for all internal transactions.
- **Velocity Siphoning**: Continuous execution of inter-node settlements to maximize the number of reward-generating events.
- **Reward Optimization**: Routing all multi-chain bridging and rebalancing flows through Fluidity-enabled channels to capture velocity deltas.
