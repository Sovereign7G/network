# 🇦🇩 Andorra‘s Digital Economy & Investment Landscape (2026)

Nestled in the Pyrenees between France and Spain, Andorra represents one of Europe’s most **deliberately architected and institution‑led digital economies**—a nation of approximately 90,000 people that has chosen **precision over scale, integration over disruption, and sustainability over speculation**. For an investor with "millions," Andorra offers a distinctive proposition: a **10% corporate income tax cap** (among the lowest in Europe), a **financial centre that has embedded fintech**, a **DTA with Estonia signed** (2025) and negotiations with Japan reportedly initiated, a **€500,000 Business Digitalisation Programme** for 2026, and an **e‑commerce market of 174 live Shopify stores** growing at 5% year‑on‑year.

Unlike the startup-led ecosystems of larger markets, Andorra is **bank‑led and integration‑focused**. Institutions such as **Andbank and Crèdit Andorrà** are absorbing fintech into their core offerings.

## 💵 Crypto Regulation: EU‑Aligned, DAC8‑Ready from 2026

**Critical Finding**: In October 2025, Andorra updated its automatic exchange agreement with the EU to include **digital assets, e‑money, and crypto‑assets**, effective **1 January 2026**.

### The Andorra‑EU Automatic Information Exchange Framework

- **Entry into force**: 1 January 2026.
- **Scope**: Financial accounts, e‑money, and crypto‑assets.
- **Reporting**: Aligned with OECD CRS and EU DAC8 standards.

**Verdict**: Andorra offers a **fully operational, EU‑aligned crypto reporting regime**.
