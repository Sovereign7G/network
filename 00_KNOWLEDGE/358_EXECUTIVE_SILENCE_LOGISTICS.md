# 🤫 [358] Executive Silence: The "OmniForward" Plan

## 🏛️ Rationale
"Executive Silence" is the peak operational mode of the Republic's logistics. It combines virtual addresses, nominee shippers, and multi-hop forwarding into a single "stateless" product. It ensures that the Republic's physical goods do not legally exist until they reach their terminal.

## 🛠️ Components
*   **Silence**: No human interaction, no public records.
*   **Security**: ZK-proof locker handshakes.
*   **Sovereignty**: Assets are held by DAOs and moved via nomads.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Total Obscurity**: The goal is not just to avoid tax, but to avoid existence in the state's commercial memory.
2.  **Absolute Privacy**: Privacy is achieved when the logistics layer is as silent as the financial ledger.
