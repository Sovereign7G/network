# 📜 [401] Republic Constitution: Cognitive Governance Layer

## 🏛️ Rationale
To govern the **Cognitive Refinery** and its refined outputs, the **Age Republic** establishes this formal Constitution. This is not a model-driven policy, but a fixed, auditable set of rules for the arbitration, verification, and hardening of intelligence. This layer ensures that the Republic achieves super-human correctness through a sovereign supply chain, not just a super-human model.

---

## I. Article I: The Veracity of the Interface
1.  **Embodied Proof**: No cognitive output shall be deemed "Refined" until it has been tested against its actual external interface (HTTP, CLI, Filesystem, or Ledger).
2.  **Anti-Simulation**: Passing internal test suites is insufficient. The Republic rejects "Simulated Correctness" in favor of "Embodied Correctness."

## II. Article II: Temporal Sovereignty
1.  **Temporal Primacy**: Coordination logic (leases, timeouts, concurrency) is the highest-stakes subproblem.
2.  **Hardening Mandate**: All temporal logic must be verified via formal property suites (TLA+ or equivalent) and counterexample traces before finality.

## III. Article III: The Arbitration of Disagreement
1.  **Multi-Manifold Dispute Resolution**: When cognitive manifolds (Claude, DeepSeek, Kimi) disagree, the region of spec is flagged as "Inherently Ambiguous."
2.  **The Constitution as Mediator**: Disagreements are resolved by the **Arbitration Layer** based on historical benchmark performance per category, not by a single model's vote.

## IV. Article IV: The Hallucination Tax & Transparency
1.  **The Ledger of Errors**: The Republic shall maintain a public, bit-verifiable ledger of known model failure modes.
2.  **The Tax**: Transparency is the currency of trust. Access to the **Hallucination Ledger** is a utility provided by the Republic to the global commons.

## V. Article V: Bit-Verifiable Intelligence
1.  **Cryptographic Attestation**: Every refined output must be hashed and signed, providing a bit-verifiable proof of hardening.
2.  **Sovereign Finality**: The Republic's attestation is the ultimate finality gate for reasoning in high-stakes domains (Legal, Financial, Infra).

---

## VI. The Transcendent Goal: Intelligence as Utility
The Republic's mission is to move refined intelligence from a proprietary product to a **Sovereign Utility**—as reliable as electricity and as transparent as mathematics.
