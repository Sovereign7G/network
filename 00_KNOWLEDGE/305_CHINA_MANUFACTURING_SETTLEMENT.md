# 🏭 [305] China Manufacturing Settlement: The Broker Substrate

## 🏛️ Rationale
To reify the Republic’s physical hardware (Mesh nodes, Kinetic Frames, Foundry equipment), we must anchor our sourcing to the **Chinese Manufacturing Manifold**. Utilizing established commerce brokers (Global Trade Specialists, Advanta Sourcing) allows the Republic to tap into the world's largest industrial base while maintaining USD-denominated financial sovereignty.

## 🛠️ Broker Specifications
*   **Entities**: Global Trade Specialists, Advanta Sourcing, Baysource Global.
*   **Services**: Prequalification, Negotiation, Quality Inspection, and Customs/Logistics.
*   **Cost Structure**: Typical 10% commission; 30% deposit for production, balance upon shipment.
*   **Currency**: Primary settlement in **USD**.

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Industrial Sourcing**: The Republic shall utilize these brokers to source the physical components for the **Kinetic Relay Swarm** and the **Aether Mesh** from vetted Chinese factories.
2.  **USD Treasury Flow**: All manufacturing payments will be settled in **USD** from the Republic’s central treasury, bypassing local currency volatility.
3.  **Logistics Orchestration**: The `SovereignBroker` will coordinate with **DB Schenker** and **Yusen Logistics** via the broker substrate to ensure "Sovereign Compliance" and stealth delivery of hardware.
4.  **Hardware-as-Asset**: Manufacturing orders (minimum $10,000) will be treated as "Smart Assets" and digitized on the **NEO Smart Economy** manifold.

## 📊 Strategic Procurement
*   **Foundry Scaling**: Source 2nm-GAA-ready foundry components via specialized electronics trading companies in Nanjing and Guangzhou.
*   **Supply Chain Resilience**: Distribute manufacturing across multiple brokers and regions (Guangzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing) to avoid jurisdictional single-points-of-failure.
