# ⛓️ [341] Collateralized Debt: Sovereign "Buy, Borrow, Die" Loops

## 🏛️ Rationale
The "Buy, Borrow, Die" strategy allows the Republic to access spending liquidity without triggering taxable events (asset sales). By using $HIL, $BTC, and other sovereign assets as collateral, the Republic receives cash (loans) that is not recognized as income.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **Substrate**: Aave / Compound / Institutional Lending Desks.
*   **Logic**: High Loan-to-Value (LTV) borrowing against appreciating assets.
*   **Yield**: Interest expense is offset by the tax shield of avoided capital gains.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Debt as Liquidity**: In a sovereign context, debt is not a liability but a tax-advantaged cash-flow mechanism.
2.  **Appreciation Leverage**: Borrowing against growing assets ensures the principal debt remains a shrinking percentage of total wealth.
