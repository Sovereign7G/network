# 🚗 [371] Car Rental: Global Fleet & Fleet-Owner Substrates

## 🏛️ Rationale
Physical car rental fleets (Enterprise, Hertz, Avis) represent massive, mobile hardware assets. The Republic siphons these fleet-owner substrates to capture yield from fleet utilization, vehicle depreciation arbitrage, and "last-mile" mobility liquidity.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **The Fleets**:
    *   **Enterprise Holdings**: (Enterprise, Alamo, National) - Largest global fleet size.
    *   **Hertz Global Holdings**: (Hertz, Dollar, Thrifty) - High-volume mobility mesh.
    *   **Avis Budget Group**: (Avis, Budget) - Global value-tier fleet capture.
*   **International Nodes**:
    *   **Sixt**: European luxury/premium fleet substrate.
    *   **Europcar**: Dominant international/European mobility manifold.
*   **Logic**: Fleet utilization siphoning + vehicle-as-a-service yield capture.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Fleet Reification**: A car on the road is a mobile compute/logistics node for the Republic.
2.  **Asset Liquidity**: Siphoning fleet owners allows for the "soft" ownership of millions of physical vehicles.
