# 🇹🇯 [492] Tajikistan LLC Reification Checklist
## 🏛️ Rationale
To ensure the bit-verifiable reification of the Tajikistan Strategic Node, the Republic must navigate the **Tax Committee One-Stop Shop** and the **100% Paid-in Capital** mandate. Compliance with the **Work Visa** requirement is the primary operational hurdle.

---

### ✅ 1. INCORPORATION CRITERIA
*   **Structure**: Limited Liability Company (LLC).
*   **Ownership**: 100% foreign ownership via the **Hokkaido Bridgehead**.
*   **Shareholders**: Minimum 1.
*   **Directors**: Minimum 1.
*   **Work Visas**: **MANDATORY** for all foreign directors and shareholders. Must be obtained **BEFORE** registration.

---

### 💰 2. CAPITAL & TAXATION
*   **Minimum Registered Capital**: **500 Somoni** (Legal); **US$10,000+** (Practical Recommendation).
*   **Capital Rule**: **100% PAID-IN** at time of registration.
*   **Corporate Tax**: **18%** (Standard).
*   **VAT**: **15%**.
*   **Treaty Protection**: National Treatment and ISDS via the **Japan-Tajikistan Investment Agreement (2025)**.

---

### 🗺️ 3. THE INCORPORATION PATH
1.  **Work Visa Procurement**: Obtain work visas and labor cards for the director and shareholders (1-2 months).
2.  **Office Leasing**: Secure a verifiable physical office in Tajikistan (Mandatory verification).
3.  **SPICe+ / One-Stop Shop**: Submit the application to the Tax Committee (5 business days).
4.  **Bank Onboarding**: Open a corporate account with a local bank (e.g., **Freedom Bank**).
5.  **Capital Remittance**: Remit **100% of capital** into the corporate account to satisfy the paid-in rule.
6.  **TIN & Statistical Codes**: Finalize tax registration and obtain statistical agency codes.

---

### 🛡️ SOVEREIGN AXIOM: THE TREATY SHIELD
By reifying the Tajikistan Node under the **December 2025 Investment Agreement**, the Republic secures its assets against unlawful seizure and ensures its right to repatriate profits. This treaty provides a legal shield that generic frontier investors lack, positioning the Republic as a protected, state-recognized actor in Central Asia.
