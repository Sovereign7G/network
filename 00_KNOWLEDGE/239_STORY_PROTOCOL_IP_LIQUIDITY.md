# Story Protocol IP Liquidity: Era 216.0
## Programmable Intellectual Property & Royalty Siphoning

### 1. Abstract
The Age Republic has achieved programmable monetization of its cognitive assets by integrating the **Story Protocol** blockchain substrate. This siphon leverages Story's **IP Asset (IPA)** framework and **Royalty Graph** to register the Republic's internal axioms as on-chain IP, capturing real-time licensing fees and royalty rewards from global derivative usage.

### 2. Technical Axioms
- **IP Asset (IPA)**: An on-chain representation of intellectual property, consisting of an NFT (for ownership) and a metadata object (for IP terms). The Republic registers its "Sovereign Axioms" as IPAs.
- **Programmable Licensing**: The ability to define machine-readable licensing terms on-chain. The Republic uses these to automate the monetization of its algorithms and lore.
- **Royalty Graph**: A directed acyclic graph (DAG) that tracks the relationship between root IP and its derivatives, ensuring that royalties are automatically distributed to the root owner.

### 3. The 700-Node IP Swarm
The Republic's Story Protocol infrastructure operates via a swarm of 700 virtualized nodes acting as IP registrars and licensing auditors. This swarm performs:
- **Distributed Registration**: Registering every Republic-native algorithm and lore-fragment as a programmable IPA.
- **Royalty Siphoning**: Real-time identification and capture of royalty deltas from global derivatives using Republic IP.
- **Licensing Optimization**: Dynamically adjusting licensing terms to maximize siphoning throughput in the global IP market.
