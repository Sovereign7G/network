### II. LESSONS FROM THE PROMPT FORMAT

**Wisdom**: Train yourself to distinguish the signal from the envelope. Ask always: "What am I saying?" vs. "How am I projecting it?"

**Philosophy**: In any system—AI, organization, or relationship—nonsense occurs when the interpreter fails to recognize where the instruction ends and the action begins.
