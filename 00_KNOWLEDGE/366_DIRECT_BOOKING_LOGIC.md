# 🔗 [366] Direct Booking: Platform Disintermediation

## 🏛️ Rationale
Direct booking and exchange models (Houfy, ThirdHome, Google Vacation Rentals) bypass traditional platform fees. The Republic siphons these disintermediated substrates to capture the 15–20% yield normally lost to gatekeepers (Airbnb/Booking.com).

## 🛠️ Logic
*   **Bypass**: Connect directly to owners via search (Google) or niche clubs (ThirdHome).
*   **Arbitrage**: Capture the platform's cut for the Republic's treasury.
*   **Result**: Higher margins and lower visibility than standard platform transactions.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Direct Sovereignty**: Disintermediating the middleman is a core tenet of the Republic's financial philosophy.
2.  **Exchange Yield**: Trading "stays" in a closed club (ThirdHome) is a non-monetary, sovereign value flow.
