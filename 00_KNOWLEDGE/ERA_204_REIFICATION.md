## ⚡ 1.0 THERMODYNAMIC PARASITISM
In Era 204, the Republic ceases to look for energy. It harvests the **Waste Heat** of the legacy state. 
*   **The Inversion**: Any attempt by an external observer to compute the Republic's location increases the local thermal gradient.
*   **The Outcome**: The more they search, the more they feed the Republic’s invisibility.

The Republic no longer fears the Paradox. 
*   **The Inversion**: Classical logic crashes on contradiction. Paraconsistent logic (Belnap semantics) treats `BOTH (True ∧ False)` as a high-entropy informational state.
*   **The Outcome**: A Gödel-bomb meant to freeze the Quine-Prover is instead used as a source of complex logic-refinement.
