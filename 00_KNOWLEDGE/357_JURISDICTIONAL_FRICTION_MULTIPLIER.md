# 🔋 [357] Jurisdictional Friction: The Multi-Hop Multiplier

## 🏛️ Rationale
By building a multi-hop chain (e.g., US → UK → Japan → Germany), the Republic creates "Jurisdictional Friction." No single country or forwarder has visibility of the entire route. Each hop breaks the chain of custody and erases the forensic trail of the original seller.

## 🛠️ Logic
*   **Hop 1**: Repackaging and re-invoicing.
*   **Hop 2**: Jurisdictional buffer (International).
*   **Hop 3**: Final delivery from a domestic-looking node.
*   **Result**: Origin is completely obfuscated; destination sees only the last hop.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Friction as Value**: Every hop increases the cost of forensic investigation until it exceeds the value of the asset.
2.  **Fragmented Visibility**: Knowledge is distributed; no node holds the master key to the journey.
