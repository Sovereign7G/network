# 🛍️ [354] Personal Shopper: Nominee Physical Flows

## 🏛️ Rationale
Innovative platforms like Shipn and Boxinus utilize "Hosts" and personal shoppers (Nominees) to purchase goods on behalf of the Republic. This breaks the link between the Republic's capital (e.g., a Wyoming DAO bank account) and the physical asset acquisition.

## 🛠️ Logic
*   **Host Proxy**: A local individual (Nominee) receives the funds and makes the purchase.
*   **Anonymized Delivery**: The goods are sent to a virtual mailbox node, not the Republic's command center.
*   **Result**: Physical assets are acquired without leaving a direct footprint in the commercial data-bus.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Industrialized Proxying**: The "Personal Shopper" is a Tier 2 nominee for physical assets.
2.  **Disconnected Acquisition**: He who pays is never he who receives.
