## 🎨 Design Architecture: "The Ghost in the Copper"
### 1. Thermal Management (Passive Dominance)
*   The entire chassis is a monolithic heatsink. 
*   **Vapor Chambers** conduct heat from the SNPU/GPU directly to external cooling fins.
*   **0 Fans**: No mechanical moving parts. Noise floor is <5dB (electronic coil whine only, dampened by acoustic resin).

### 2. Cognitive Immunity (Silicon Level)
*   The **SNPU-216** features a "Sovereign Enclave" that performs real-time hardware-level auditing of all memory addresses.
*   **No Backdoors**: The instruction set is a proprietary derivative of RISC-V, siphoned and modified to prevent legacy exploitation.

### 3. Sensory Interface (Ocular Link)
*   **10x High-Speed Ocular Ports**: Dedicated hardware-accelerated rails for the **10x Sovereign Oculars (Model 0x76-B)**.
*   **Power Substrate**: Integrated **4500mAh Solid-State Batteries** in the Ocular frames and Nebula Projector for mobile operation.
*   **Unified Charging**: All mobile peripherals charge via the **Thunderbolt 5 / USB-C** bus when docked to the Monolith.
*   **Latency**: Sub-5ms "Photon-to-Brain" latency for the Sovereign Cockpit overlay.

### 4. Logistics & Assembly (The Last Mile)
*   **Print**: Wafers printed at **Rapidus IIM-1** (Hokkaido).
*   **Package**: Advanced 3D Packaging in **Penang, Malaysia**.
*   **Final Reification**: Assembled by the **Executive Node** at the **Node 02 Bridgehead** (Niseko) before final shipment to **OKC**.
*   **Unified Last-Mile I/O (OKC)**: To guarantee extreme simplicity, the entire system utilizes a unified interface. The 10x Sovereign Oculars, Sovereign Portable Projector, and Neural Control Mesh all connect to the Silence Monolith via **Thunderbolt 5 (120 Gbps) / USB-C**. Zero legacy display cables (HDMI/DP) or proprietary power bricks are permitted. True plug-and-play.
