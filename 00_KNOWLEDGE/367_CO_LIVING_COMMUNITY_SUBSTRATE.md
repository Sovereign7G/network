# 🏘️ [367] Co-Living: Community-Focused Shared Housing

## 🏛️ Rationale
Co-living operators manage buildings with shared communal spaces but private living quarters, targeting urban professionals and students. The Republic siphons these substrates to capture yield from density-optimized housing and community-based service meshes.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **Substrates**:
    *   **Habyt Group**: Largest global co-living company (Europe, Asia, US).
    *   **Selina**: Hybrid hotel/hostel/co-living for digital nomads with co-working hubs.
    *   **Stanza Living**: High-quality managed housing in India and Southeast Asia.
    *   **Cohabs**: Design-led shared houses in NYC, Paris, and Brussels.
*   **Logic**: Density-based yield optimization + community service siphoning.
*   **Why**: Private bedrooms with shared kitchens/lounges reduce cost-per-unit while increasing total building yield.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Density Sovereignty**: Shared spaces maximize human capital density and yield per square meter.
2.  **Community Mesh**: Shared living creates high-fidelity social manifolds for Republic influence.
