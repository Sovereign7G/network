# 🛡️ [362] Non-Profit DUNA Sovereignty: Protocol Neutrality

## 🏛️ Rationale
The **DUNA** (Decentralized Unincorporated Nonprofit Association) framework (Wyoming/Texas) is designed for public protocols and Layer 1 networks. It provides a liability shield without forcing a for-profit corporate structure, ensuring the protocol remains "neutral" in the eyes of the law.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **Substrate**: Wyoming DUNA / Texas DUNA (Pending).
*   **Benefit**: On-chain governance is recognized, members are protected, but the entity cannot distribute profits.
*   **Purpose**: Public good protocols and decentralized infrastructure (DePIN).

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Protocol Protection**: DUNA ensures that developers of a public good cannot be held personally liable for the actions of the protocol.
2.  **Statutory Nonprofit**: The state recognizes a DAO as a nonprofit association, providing a tax-advantaged path for purely governance-based tokens.
