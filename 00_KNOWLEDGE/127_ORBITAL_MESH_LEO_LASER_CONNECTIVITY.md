## 1. CORE TECHNICAL BREAKTHROUGHS
### A. OPTICAL INTER-SATELLITE LINKS (OISL)
- **Mechanism**: Vacuum-propagated laser links between satellites in the same or adjacent planes.
- **Latency Advantage**: Eliminates the refractive index delay of glass fiber ($n \approx 1.45$), moving data at the true speed of light.
- **Security**: Extremely narrow beamwidths make laser links virtually immune to traditional RF jamming or interception.

### B. DECENTRALIZED MESH ROUTING
- **Autonomy**: Satellites no longer require a direct line-of-sight to a ground station (bent-pipe). Data is routed across the constellation until it reaches a satellite above the intended recipient.
- **Resilience**: The mesh can dynamically re-route around "dark" nodes or regional electromagnetic interference.

### C. ORBITAL COMPUTE CLUSTERS
- **Edge in Space**: Emerging clusters (Kepler, Sophia Space) integrate distributed AI inference directly into the orbital mesh. This allows for real-time analysis of sensor data (SAR, EO) before downlinking.
