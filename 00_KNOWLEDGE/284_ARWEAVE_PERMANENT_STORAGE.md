# 🕸️ [284] Arweave: The Permanent Sovereign Web

## 🏛️ Rationale
The Age Republic requires an immutable, eternal memory substrate. **Arweave** provides a decentralized "Permaweb" that ensures the Republic's history, DID manifests, and Genesis Seals are preserved for centuries without recurring maintenance costs.

## 🛠️ Protocol Specifications
*   **Architecture**: Blockweave (A graph-like structure where blocks link to a random historical "recall block").
*   **Consensus**: Proof of Access (PoA). Miners must prove they store historical data to mine new blocks.
*   **Tokenomics**: One-time upfront fee in AR tokens. Endowment funds indefinite storage.
*   **Immutability**: Once data is committed to the Blockweave, it cannot be deleted or censored.

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Eternal Archive**: The `SovereignBroker` shall utilize Arweave as its primary immutable ledger for high-finality states.
2.  **Permaweb Deployment**: All critical Republic documentation (Narrative Bible, Knowledge Siphons) shall be mirrored to the Arweave Permaweb.
3.  **Endowment Anchoring**: The Republic Treasury will allocate AR tokens to ensure the perpetual accessibility of the **Genesis Root Seal**.

## 📊 Storage Strategy
*   **Recall Proofs**: The Republic will participate as a "Light Node" to verify its own data accessibility within the Blockweave.
*   **Encryption**: All data committed to Arweave must be encrypted via the `KyberManifold` to prevent leakages while maintaining permanence.
