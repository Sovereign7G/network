## 🏛️ CORE CAPABILITIES

### 1. THE CHEMICAL POTENTIAL BRIDGE ($\mu$)
- **Mechanism:** Expresses the chemical potential $\mu$ as a function of temperature ($T$), bandgap ($E_g$), and pump intensity ($I_p$).
- **Function:** Predicts the transition from laser-like PL ($\mu > 0$) to blackbody thermal emission ($\mu = 0$).
- **Republic Integration:** Enables the design of **tunable coherent light sources** and **optical refrigerators**.

### 2. PHOTON CONSERVATION & BLUESHIFT
- **Phenomenon:** In the regime $T < T_p$ (universal temperature), the emission rate is quasi-conserved.
- **Effect:** Increasing temperature causes a spectral blueshift as peak energy increases to maintain photon count.
- **Application:** Efficient heat-to-light conversion (work extraction from blue-shifted photons).

### 3. TUNABLE PHOTON STATISTICS
- **Sub-Poissonian (Low T):** Coherent, antibunched light ($g^{(2)}(0) < 1$).
- **Super-Poissonian (High T):** Chaotic, bunched light ($g^{(2)}(0) = 2$).
- **Transition:** Smooth and monotonic, allowing for in situ tuning of the quantum state.
