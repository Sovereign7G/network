## 🧩 The Fragmented Landscape

The decentralized landscape contains projects building parts of this vision, but none offer a unified trust-minimized multi-chain settlement and fiat rail engine.

### 1. Settlement & Attestation Layers

Projects building trust-minimized settlement verification are typically chain-bound or limited in scope:

* **LayerEdge:** Focuses on trust-minimized settlement for Bitcoin using zero-knowledge proof (ZKP) aggregation. While it reduces costs on Bitcoin, it is chain-specific and does not verify multi-chain DePIN/AI operations.
* **Zulu Network:** Implements a two-tiered Bitcoin architecture for DePIN/AI but remains Bitcoin-centric.

### 2. "Universal" Settlement Layers

General-purpose cross-chain settlement protocols focus on user experience and liquidity, not infrastructure validation:

* **Particle Chain:** Serves as a coordination layer for chain abstraction and Universal Accounts. It aggregates liquidity but does not provide DePIN/AI verification or ICP attestation.
* **Push Chain:** A shared-state layer for cross-chain app coordination rather than resource/settlement validation.

### 3. DePIN + AI + Settlement Prototypes

* **Autheo:** A full Layer-0 OS with native compute/storage and sovereign identity. However, it requires a bespoke Proof-of-Authority runtime instead of wrapping existing networks with Base/ICP.
* **Vocaid Hub:** A hackathon prototype protocol using ERC-8004 registries on 0G Chain to verify hardware, but relies on Hedera for settlement and is not production-ready.
* **PinGo + Manadia:** A single-protocol integration tracking compute resources on-chain without general-purpose multi-chain settlement corridors.

### 4. Niche Settlement Protocols

* **DeDe Protocol:** Logistics-centric settlement protocol for parcel deliveries on Ethereum Mainnet.
