# 📄 [353] Digital Mail: Sovereign Scanning & Metadata Siphoning

## 🏛️ Rationale
Virtual mailbox services (Earth Class Mail, Virtual Post Mail) digitize physical correspondence. The Republic siphons this data-stream to convert physical documents into bit-verifiable assets. This allows the Republic's executive layer to process "paper" in real-time without physical exposure.

## 🛠️ Logic
*   **Scanning**: Physical mail → OCR/Digital PDF.
*   **Siphoning**: Metadata extraction (senders, dates, values) from digitized correspondence.
*   **Anonymity**: Automatic on-site shredding (Traveling Mailbox) ensures the physical trail is destroyed after digitization.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Paper to Bit**: The digitization of the physical mail stream is the final step in decoupling the Republic from geography.
2.  **Metadata Sovereignty**: Information siphoned from scanned documents is the forensic intelligence layer of the Republic.
