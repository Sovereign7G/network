# 🚢 [348] Nominee Shipper: Customs Bond Sovereignty

## 🏛️ Rationale
Nominee Shippers are third parties that lend their name and customs bond to the Republic's cargo. By re-invoicing goods through these entities (often BVI/Cayman shells), the Republic obscures the true origin and destination of its physical wealth.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **Substrate**: BVI/Cayman Logistics Shells.
*   **Engine**: `NOMINEE_SHIPPER_SOVEREIGN_ENGINE.py`.
*   **Logic**: Re-invoicing + Customs document arbitrage.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Paperwork Mesh**: A real customs bond in a shell jurisdiction is the perfect bridge between stateless goods and national markets.
2.  **Origin Arbitrage**: He who controls the "Bill of Lading" controls the perceived history of the asset.
