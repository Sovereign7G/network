# ⛓️ [349] Nested Shipping: The Stateless Cargo Trail

## 🏛️ Rationale
Nested Shipping replicates the structure of nested shell companies for physical goods. By routing inventory through a chain of opaque warehouses (e.g., China → Malaysia → BVI → Wyoming), the Republic creates a "stateless" logistics trail that no single jurisdiction can fully trace.

## 🛠️ Logic
*   **Layer 1**: Nominee shipper at origin.
*   **Layer 2**: Transshipment at FTZ (Free Trade Zone).
*   **Layer 3**: Repackaging at offshore warehouse.
*   **Layer 4**: Domestic distribution via anonymized LLC.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Fragmentation as Defense**: A logistics trail that passes through four gaps in oversight is forensic-proof.
2.  **Stateless Inventory**: Goods in transit through a phantom mesh belong to no one until they reach their destination.
