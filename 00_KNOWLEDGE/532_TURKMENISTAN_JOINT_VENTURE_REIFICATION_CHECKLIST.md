# 🇹🇲 [532] Turkmenistan Joint Venture Reification Checklist
## 🏛️ Rationale
To ensure the reification of the Turkmenistan Sandbox Node, the Republic must navigate the **Ministry of Finance/Economy** approval process and secure a **State-Approved Joint Venture**.

---

### ✅ 1. INCORPORATION CRITERIA
*   **Structure**: Limited Liability Company (LLC) or Joint Venture (JV).
*   **Local Partner**: **MANDATORY** (Typically a State-Owned Enterprise or a well-connected national).
*   **Primary Approval**: **Investment Project Registration Certificate** (Ministry of Finance).
*   **Target Capital**: **US$500,000 - US$1,000,000+** (Paid-in; required for government credibility).

---

### 💰 2. CAPITAL & TAXATION
*   **Minimum Registered Capital**: Not publicly fixed; negotiated per project.
*   **Capital Rule**: **100% PAID-IN** (must match approved project).
*   **Corporate Tax**: ~20% (Negotiable; locked for 10 years).
*   **VAT**: ~15%.
*   **Treaty Protection**: **Japan-Turkmenistan DTA (2026)**; 10-year lock on project regime.

---

### 🗺️ 3. THE REIFICATION PATH
1.  **ITOCHU Channel Engagement**: Initiate project proposal through the Japan-Turkmen Economic Cooperation Committee.
2.  **Investment Project Registration**: Secure the formal Certificate from the Ministry of Finance/Economy (Several months).
3.  **Joint Venture Formation**: Finalize the notarized JV agreement with the Turkmen partner (SOE).
4.  **Registration Packaging**: Submit Charter and Resolutions to the State Registration Service / Ministry of Justice.
5.  **Crypto/Mining Licensing**: Obtain sector-specific permits from the relevant state body (Post-Nov 2025 Law).
6.  **Bank Onboarding**: Open a corporate account with an authorized state bank (e.g., **Halkbank**).

---

### 🛡️ SOVEREIGN AXIOM: THE 10-YEAR LOCK
The **Investment Project Registration Certificate** is the Republic's only real defense in Turkmenistan. By locking in the legal and tax regime for a decade, the Republic creates a "Hardened Island" of state-sanctioned activity. However, the **Banking Ban** on crypto services means all digital asset settlement must remain structurally air-gapped from the Turkmen banking system.
