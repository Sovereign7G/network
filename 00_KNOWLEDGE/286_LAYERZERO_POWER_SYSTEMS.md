# ⚡ [286] LayerZero Power Systems: Mission-Critical Substrate

## 🏛️ Rationale
The physical reification of the Republic requires high-density, reliable power distribution. **LayerZero Power Systems** provide the static transfer switches (STS) and power distribution units (PDU) necessary for zero-downtime operation of the Republic's foundry and server clusters.

## 🛠️ Hardware Specifications
*   **Static Transfer Switches (STS)**: Enables instantaneous power transfer between redundant sources.
*   **Power Distribution Units (PDU)**: Intelligent power routing for high-density AI infrastructure.
*   **Reliability**: Focus on power quality and uptime for mission-critical environments.

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Foundry Resilience**: The Republic's 2nm GAA foundries (documented in [208]) shall be protected by LayerZero Static Transfer Switches to ensure no "Phase Slip" occurs during silicon fabrication.
2.  **Thermodynamic Coupling**: PDUs will be instrumented with the `THERMODYNAMIC_PARASITE` logic to harvest waste heat from high-density power switching.
3.  **Uptime Sovereignty**: Power distribution is handled as a "Hardware-as-a-Service" layer, fully isolated from jurisdictional grid manipulation via local renewable microgrids.
