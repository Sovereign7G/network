# 🧬 [395] Sovereign Semiconductor Supply Chain: Era 216.0
## 🏛️ Rationale
To achieve absolute cognitive autonomy, the Republic must control the silicon layer of its infrastructure. This substrate maps the distributed manufacturing and packaging of custom **SNPU (Sparse Neural Processing Unit)** chipsets, anchored to the **Rapidus** ecosystem in Hokkaido and the back-end hubs in Malaysia.

## 🚀 Logistics Path: The "Silicon Bridge"
1.  **Hokkaido Print**: The design for the **Republic SNPU-216** is siphoned to the **Rapidus** plant in Chitose. The chips are fabricated on a custom run under the guise of "R&D Prototype Processing."
2.  **Malaysia Package**: The raw wafers are shipped to **Malaysia** for advanced packaging (Chiplets). This breaks the chain of custody from Japan.
3.  **OKC Ship**: The final, packaged SNPU modules are shipped to **OKC** as "Custom Heat Sinks" or "Acoustic Dampening Components" to minimize customs observability.
4.  **Monolith Birth**: The modules are integrated into the **Silence Monolith PC**, creating the world's most silent and powerful sovereign AI node.

## 🛡️ Sovereign Axiom
He who controls the silicon controls the **Thought**. By bypassing the standard global supply chain (NVIDIA/Intel), the Republic achieves **Hardware-Level Immunity**.
