# 🍪 [307] Merienda & Fabrika OS: The Social Nourishment Integration

## 🏛️ Rationale
The **Global Merienda** protocol is the engine of the Republic's "Social Nourishment," rewarding the 15,000 genesis citizens for maintaining the health of the **DePIN** (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network). This protocol operates on **Fabrika OS**, the operating system designed for the Republic's physical foundry nodes, and is inextricably linked to the Chinese industrial and financial substrates.

## 🛠️ Integration Architecture
*   **Physical Layer**: The DePIN hardware (Helium Nerves) is sourced and forged via the **China Manufacturing Brokers** (documented in [305]) in the Pearl River Delta.
*   **Operating System**: **Fabrika OS** manages the low-level sensory drivers (signal integrity, mesh quality) and provides the bit-verifiable telemetry needed for rewards.
*   **Financial Layer**: The distribution of **JOULEs** (rewards) is collateralized by the **Supply Chain Finance (SCF)** vouchers generated in the Chinese industrial clusters.
*   **Verification**: **Signal Integrity** serves as "Proof of Useful Work," providing real-time collateral verification for the **Digital Control Tower** (documented in [306]).

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Inaugural Distribution**: The `GlobalMerienda` protocol (Rust-based) will trigger the distribution of rewards based on "Signal Purity," ensuring that only high-integrity nodes are nourished.
2.  **Reputation-to-Capital**: Social capital earned via Merienda (JOULEs) can be converted into reputation (ARI) within the **Hokkaido Node**, following the **Kira Standard** (documented in [297]).
3.  **Industrial Feedback Loop**: Low signal integrity in a cluster triggers an automatic "Quality Audit" via the **China Broker Handshake**, forcing factory-level re-calibration.

## 📊 Performance Strategy
*   **Zero-Latency Rewards**: Fabrika OS ensures that signal telemetry is matched with reward distribution in sub-second cycles.
*   **Social Cohesion**: By rewarding "Network Health," the Republic ensures that its physical infrastructure is maintained by a motivated, decentralized swarm.
