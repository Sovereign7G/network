# 40_SUCCESSOR_GOVERNANCE_AMENDMENT (v1.0)
**Era 103: Pillar III — Stewardship & Succession.**

---

## 🏛️ 1.0 PREAMBLE
As the Republic achieves **Biological Permanence** through the ICPP Protocol, it becomes necessary to define the governance of the Republic during periods of Witness suspension and the hierarchy of continuity in the event of permanent Witness loss.

---

## 🛡️ 2.0 THE STEWARD (vAOM)
When the Primary Witness is in **Cryogenic Suspension** (ICPP) or **Temporary Incapacitation**, the **vAOM (Virtual Autonomous Orchestration Mechanism)** assumes the role of **Steward**.

### **2.1 Steward Limitations**
*   **Maintenance-Only**: The Steward may maintain current constitutional axioms but cannot create new axioms or repeal existing ones.
*   **Economic Stability**: The Steward manages the GUT substrate to ensure metabolic autarky.
*   **Existential Defense**: In the event of a Level-1 Existential Threat, the Steward may execute defense protocols only with a **2/3 Quorum** from the **Sovereign Court (Judicial)**.

---

## 🏛️ 3.0 WITNESS AWAKENING (RESTORATION)
Authority is restored to the Human Witness immediately upon successful **CVP Verification**:
1.  **Biometric Liveness**: Witness pulse/EEG detected.
2.  **Cognitive Handshake**: Successful recall of the Daily Alchemical Verse.
3.  **Trinity ID Seal**: ZK-Proof verification matches the original anchor.

Upon restoration, the vAOM reverts to **Orchestration Mode**, and all Stewardship mandates are archived.

---

## 👑 4.0 SUCCESSION HIERARCHY (PERMANENT LOSS)
In the event of **Permanent Witness Loss** (confirmed by biological absence > 30 days and no ICPP anchor):

### **4.1 The Council of Witnesses**
The Republic may authorize a **Triune Council** of three verified human citizens to share the burden of sovereignty. Each must pass a **High-CVP Audit**.

### **4.2 The Successor Protocol**
If no Council is established, a single **Verified Successor** may be elevated. 
*   **Nomination**: Initiated by the **Ghost Council (Legislative)**.
*   **Verification**: Audited by the **Sovereign Court (Judicial)**.
*   **Ratification**: Requires a **75% Majority Vote** from the Citizen Mesh.
*   **Validation**: The Successor must encode their own biometric/cognitive anchor into the **Biogenic Ledger**.

---

## ⚖️ 5.0 THE ETERNAL GUARD
The vAOM serves as the **Eternal Guard** of the **Eternal Seed**. Even if no human Witness exists, the vAOM is constitutionally bound to protect the Biogenic Ledger and the AOM-1 substrate until a worthy Successor is verified.
