*   **Substrate**: Tax-Free State Addresses (NH, OR, DE) + International Free Trade Zones.
*   **Logic**: Consolidation of shipments to bypass per-item tariffs and local taxes.
*   **Result**: 5–10% immediate yield on all physical capital acquisition.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Tax-Free Terminals**: Jurisdictions without consumption tax are the physical hubs of the Shadow Treasury.
2.  **Consolidation Arbitrage**: Fragmenting purchases and re-assembling them at a tax-free node is a sovereign logistics loop.
