# 🖨️ [308] Fabrika: Sovereign Hardware Synthesis Engine

## 🏛️ Rationale
To achieve "Total Material Autonomy," the Age Republic must automate the design and manufacturing of its hardware. **Fabrika** is a deterministic hardware synthesis engine (inspired by LEAP71) that translates high-level specifications into print-ready geometries (GDSII, 3D print profiles). It ensures that every component of the Republic is bit-verifiable and economically settled via a dual-token model.

## 🛠️ Engine Architecture
*   **Modular Design**: 
    *   `fabrika_core.py`: Base types and deterministic engine architecture.
    *   `fabrika_audits.py`: Specialized foundry component audits (Ocean, Anti-matter, Grid).
    *   `fabrika_synthesis.py`: Fleet mission orchestration and full-stack synthesis.
*   **Dual-Token Settlement**:
    *   **MERIT Burn**: Measures the social/reputational cost of synthesis.
    *   **$HIL Payment**: The financial fuel (Hilbert tokens) required for material processing.
*   **Anchor to ICP**: All synthesis proofs are anchored to the Internet Computer (ICP) to ensure immutable audit trails of the Republic's physical expansion.

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Specification-to-Geometry**: The Republic shall use Fabrika to synthesize all critical hardware components, from **Hilbert Storm Coolers** to **TFLN Photonic Shards**, directly from the `SovereignBroker` logic.
2.  **Mission Orchestration**: Complex sovereign missions (Ocean Infrastructure, Continental Grid) will be synthesized as unified "Fleet Missions," ensuring all sub-components are harmonized by design.
3.  **Foundry Coupling**: Fabrika output is fed directly into the **SNC-Q7.2 Foundry** (integrated with Chinese industrial clusters), enabling "Zero-Latency Manufacturing" from logic to steel.

## 📊 Synthesis Domains
*   **Thermal**: Hilbert-curve heat exchangers for high-load reactors.
*   **Quantum**: Aether-Mesh backbone connectivity and TFLN photonic switches.
*   **Energy**: Anti-matter containment and continental grid management.
*   **Aquatic**: Sovereign ocean infrastructure (SOI) for maritime autonomy.
