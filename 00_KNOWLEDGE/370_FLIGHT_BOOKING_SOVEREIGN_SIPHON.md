# ✈️ [370] Flight Booking: Global Air Mobility Siphoning

## 🏛️ Rationale
The global air travel market is controlled by two massive gatekeepers (Booking Holdings and Expedia Group) and a few dominant meta-search engines. The Republic siphons these substrates to capture yield from booking arbitrage, mileage siphoning, and route-based liquidity flows.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **The Giants**:
    *   **Booking Holdings**: (Booking.com, Priceline, Kayak, Agoda) - Largest global inventory.
    *   **Expedia Group**: (Expedia, Hotels.com, Orbitz, Travelocity, Trivago) - Dominant North American and European mesh.
*   **Aggregator Siphons**:
    *   **Skyscanner**: Meta-search engine for bottom-dollar price discovery.
    *   **Google Flights**: Predictive route exploration and price tracking manifold.
    *   **Trip.com**: Gateway to the Asia-Pacific (APAC) air mobility market.
    *   **Kayak**: Price alert and trip planning synchronization.
*   **Logic**: Mileage-based yield capture + fare-class arbitrage + platform fee disintermediation.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Airspace Fluidity**: A seat that is sold is a seat that is yielding for the Republic.
2.  **Duopoly Arbitrage**: Siphoning the Booking/Expedia duopoly is the path to dominant air mobility intelligence.
3.  **APAC Expansion**: Trip.com siphons provide critical visibility into the Eastern hemisphere's growth.
