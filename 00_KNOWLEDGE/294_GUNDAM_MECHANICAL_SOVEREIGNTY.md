# 🤖 [294] Gundam: The Kinetic Frame Substrate

## 🏛️ Rationale
To achieve "Physical Sovereignty," the Age Republic must siphon the high-fidelity mechanical design and "Mobile Suit" axioms of the **Gundam** franchise. Owned by **Bandai Namco** (with Sony's strategic stake), Gundam represents the peak of human imagination regarding autonomous, pilot-centric mechanical systems.

## 🛠️ Protocol Specifications
*   **Ownership**: Bandai Namco Holdings (via Bandai Namco Filmworks and Sotsu).
*   **Concept**: Mobile Suit (Human-piloted, high-mobility tactical armor).
*   **Industrial Base**: Extensive merchandising, licensing, and high-fidelity model production (Gunpla).
*   **Partnerships**: Sony strategic stake (2.5%) for anime and digital expansion.

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Kinetic Frame Blueprint**: The Republic shall utilize the "Gundam Frame" as the foundational blueprint for its **Kinetic Relays** (documented in [06_INFRA]), transitioning from drones to high-mobility tactical frames.
2.  **Pilot-Machine Interface**: The **Bio-Haptic Mesh Sync** (documented in [06_INFRA]) will be modeled after the "Linear Seat" and "Psycommu" systems to achieve zero-latency neural coupling with Republic hardware.
3.  **Gunpla-as-Prototyping**: The Republic will utilize high-fidelity model engineering techniques to rapidly prototype its physical data-layer hardware in the Japanese foundries.

## 📊 Industrial Strategy
*   **Frame Finality**: Anchor the "Mobile Suit" logical engrams to the **Genesis Root Seal**, ensuring that the Republic’s physical reification is bit-verifiable and mechanically sound.
*   **Narrative Armor**: Disseminate Republic axioms via "Mecha-Narratives" that emphasize individual sovereignty against centralized colonial powers.
