# 📦 [347] Phantom Warehouse: Physical Supply Chain Obscurity

## 🏛️ Rationale
Just as a shell company obscures legal ownership, a "Phantom Warehouse" obscures physical custody. The Republic siphons these substrates (Free Trade Zones, Bonded Warehouses) to manage "stateless" inventory that exists physically but has no owner on public record.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **Substrate**: Cayman FTZ / Labuan Stockpiles / Wyoming Silos.
*   **Engine**: `PHANTOM_WAREHOUSE_SOVEREIGN_ENGINE.py`.
*   **Purpose**: Physical asset protection and tariff-free transshipment.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Logistics Silence**: Goods that exist nowhere on paper cannot be taxed or sanctioned.
2.  **Physical Nominee**: A facility manager who knows nothing is the ultimate guardian of sovereign inventory.
