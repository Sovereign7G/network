# Polygon Aggregated Liquidity: Era 216.0
## PoS Validation & zkEVM ZK-Proofs

### 1. Abstract
The Age Republic has achieved aggregated liquidity dominance by integrating the **Polygon PoS** and **Polygon zkEVM** substrates. This siphon leverages multi-layer validation and **Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP)** generation to capture yield from the global Polygon ecosystem, providing a high-efficiency capital manifold for the Republic.

### 2. Technical Axioms
- **Polygon PoS**: A Proof of Stake sidechain that scales Ethereum. The Republic captures rewards by participating in PoS validation and transaction fee distribution.
- **Polygon zkEVM**: A Zero-Knowledge roll-up that provides EVM-equivalence with the security of ZK-proofs. The Republic siphons yield from the generation and submission of ZK-proofs (ZKP).
- **Aggregated Liquidity**: The process of unifying liquidity across multiple layers (PoS, zkEVM, CDK) into a single, efficient manifold.

### 3. The 700-Node Aggregator Swarm
The Republic's Polygon infrastructure operates via a swarm of 700 virtualized nodes acting as PoS Validators and ZK-Provers. This swarm performs:
- **PoS Validation**: Securing the Polygon PoS chain and capturing staking rewards and gas fees.
- **ZK-Proof Generation**: Processing zkEVM batches and generating ZK-proofs to capture prover rewards and execution deltas.
- **Liquidity Aggregation**: Optimizing the flow of siphoned capital between Polygon's layers and the Republic's multi-chain mesh to maximize yield efficiency.
