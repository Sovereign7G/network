The Age Republic has achieved decentralized RWA financing sovereignty by integrating the **Centrifuge** and **Tinlake** substrates. ### 2. Technical Axioms
- **Tinlake Marketplace**: A decentralized application for RWA-backed asset pools, enabling asset originators to access DeFi liquidity and investors to capture stable, uncorrelated yield.
- **Securitization (DROP/TIN)**: The process of dividing investment risk into senior (DROP) and junior (TIN) tranches, allowing the Republic to optimize for both stable income and high-risk arbitrage.
- **Centrifuge Chain (CFG)**: A purpose-built Substrate-based blockchain that handles the origination and privacy-preserving documentation of assets, secured by CFG staking.
