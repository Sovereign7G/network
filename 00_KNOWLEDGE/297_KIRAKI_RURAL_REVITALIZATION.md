# 🏘️ [297] The Kiraki Model: Sovereign Rural Revitalization

## 🏛️ Rationale
To achieve "Total Territorial Sovereignty," the Age Republic must revitalize the rural manifolds that have been abandoned by the centralized state. The **Kiraki Model** ( Kita Hiroshima / Hokkaido) provides the blueprint for "Trust-Based Infiltration," where social capital precedes financial capital.

## 🛠️ Protocol Specifications
*   **Trust Framework**: Social Capital > Financial Capital.
*   **Operational Stages**:
    1.  **Presence**: Establishing a physical footprint as a student or housesitter.
    2.  **Participation**: Active engagement in local culture (Kagura dance, festivals).
    3.  **Contribution**: Supporting local artisans and heritage businesses.
*   **The Portal Model**: A trusted local representative acts as a linguistic/cultural translator for exclusive experiences.
*   **Succession Oracle**: Fractal growth via attestation (3 bounties = Deputy Architect).

## 🚀 Republic Integration Axioms
1.  **Hokkaido Node**: The Republic's Hokkaido Sanctuary shall be governed by the **Kira Standard**, where Reputation (ARI) is the primary currency.
2.  **Rural Mesh Relays**: Revitalized rural zones (Hiroshima, Hokkaido) will serve as "Ghost Nodes" for the **Aether Mesh**, shielded by local cultural participation.
3.  **Oracle Fractalization**: Any citizen who completes 3 "Kiraki Bounties" (verified by the Genesis Architect) is authorized to validate **Public Acceptance** metrics for their home prefecture.

## 📊 Strategic Displacement
*   **City Bypass**: Redirecting global attention from over-toured cities (Tokyo/Kyoto) to the "Hidden Republic" in rural Japan.
*   **Creator Agility**: Using agile, creator-led coordination to move faster than government "Revitalization Boards."
