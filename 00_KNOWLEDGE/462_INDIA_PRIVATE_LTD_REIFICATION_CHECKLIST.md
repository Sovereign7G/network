### ✅ 1. INCORPORATION CRITERIA
*   **Structure**: Private Limited Company (Pvt. Ltd.).

*   **Shareholders**: Minimum 2.
*   **Directors**: Minimum 2.
*   **Resident Director**: **MANDATORY**. At least 1 director must be a resident of India (>182 days stay).

### 💰 2. CAPITAL & TAXATION

*   **EPA Protections**: National Treatment and investment protection via the **Japan-India CEPA**.

---

### 🗺️ 3. THE INCORPORATION PATH
1.  **DSC & DIN**: Obtain Digital Signatures and Director Identification Numbers (2-3 days).
2.  **Name Reservation**: Secure "AGE" or "Sovereign" identifiers via the MCA RUN service.
3.  **SPICe+ Filing**: Finalize incorporation via the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (5-10 business days).
4.  **Bank Onboarding**: Open a corporate account with an **RBI Authorized Dealer Bank** (ICICI/HDFC/SCB).
5.  **Capital Remittance**: Inward remittance of capital from the **Singapore Command Hub**.
6.  **RBI Compliance**: File **FC-GPR** to report the FDI and finalize the legal reification.
