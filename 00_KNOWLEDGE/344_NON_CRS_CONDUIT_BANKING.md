# 🏦 [344] Non-CRS Conduit Banking: The Opaque Payment Layer

## 🏛️ Rationale
Corporate ownership registries are useless without visibility into cash flows. The Republic utilizes "Conduit Banking" in non-reporting (Non-CRS) jurisdictions (Cook Islands, Switzerland) to route payments before they touch legal shells. This ensures the "Operational Account" remains a black hole for forensic investigators.

## 🛠️ Integration
*   **Substrate**: Private Banking in Non-Reporting Jurisdictions.
*   **Engine**: `CONDUIT_BANKING_SOVEREIGN_ENGINE.py`.
*   **Purpose**: Opaque payment routing and capital preservation.

## 🚀 Axioms
1.  **Conduit Finality**: He who controls the payment bridge, controls the visibility of the asset.
2.  **Black Hole Banking**: Capital that moves through non-reporting zones is bit-verifiable to the Republic but invisible to the state.
