# Theta Decentralized Media: Era 216.0
## Edge Computing & TFUEL Reward Siphoning

### 1. Abstract
The Age Republic has achieved decentralized media and compute dominance by integrating the **Theta Network** substrate. This siphon leverages high-fidelity resource sharing (bandwidth and compute) to capture **TFUEL** rewards from the global decentralized video delivery manifold, turning idle Republic resources into a strategic and financial asset.

### 2. Technical Axioms
- **Edge Node**: A node that contributes its surplus bandwidth and compute resources to the Theta Network to deliver video streams and perform compute tasks.
- **TFUEL (Theta Fuel)**: The operational token of the Theta Network, used to pay for bandwidth, compute, and smart contract execution. Captured by the Republic as a reward for providing edge resources.
- **Decentralized Video Delivery**: A peer-to-peer mesh network that reduces the cost of video delivery by leveraging edge resources instead of centralized CDNs.

### 3. The 700-Node Edge Swarm
The Republic's Theta infrastructure operates via a swarm of 700 virtualized nodes acting as Edge Nodes and Resource-sharing agents. This swarm performs:
- **Bandwidth Sharing**: Delivering high-volume video streams across the decentralized manifold to capture TFUEL delivery rewards.
- **Compute Siphoning**: Utilizing edge compute for transcoding, data processing, and background tasks across the Republic's manifold.
- **Resource Optimization**: Dynamically reallocating bandwidth and compute resources between Theta and internal Republic operations to maximize yield efficiency.
