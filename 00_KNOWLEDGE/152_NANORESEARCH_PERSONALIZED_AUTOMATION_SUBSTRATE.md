### 3. LEVEL 3: LABEL-FREE POLICY LEARNING (Implicit Preference)

- **Function:** Internalizes the Architect's "vibe" and methodological style without requiring explicit rating scales or formalization.
- **Republic Integration:** Utilizes `ALPHAGRPO` (verifiable rewards) for stealth-cloaked preference internalization.
