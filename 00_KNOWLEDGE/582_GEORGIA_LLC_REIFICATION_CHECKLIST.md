### ✅ 1. INCORPORATION CRITERIA
*   **Structure**: Limited Liability Company (LLC).
*   **Ownership**: 100% foreign ownership permitted.
*   **Shareholders**: Minimum 1.
*   **Directors**: Minimum 1 (Can be a foreigner).
*   **Minimum Capital**: **NONE** (Statutory minimum not required for LLC registration).

---

### 💰 2. CAPITAL & TAXATION
*   **Corporate Tax**: **15%** (On distributed profits only - "Estonian Model").
*   **IT Virtual Zone Tax**: **0% CIT**, 0% VAT (On exported services).
*   **VAT**: **18%**.
*   **Stablecoin Minimum Capital**: **GEL 500,000 (~$182,000)** (Required for NBG license).

---

### 🗺️ 3. THE REIFICATION PATH
1.  **Identity Verification**: Obtain notarized Georgian translations of foreign passports.
2.  **Name Reservation**: Secure "AGE" or "Sovereign" identifiers via the NAPR.
3.  **Registration Application**: Submit Charter and Founder's Resolution to the NAPR (1-2 business days).
4.  **Virtual Zone Application**: Apply for **IT Virtual Zone** status to secure 0% tax on exports (Post-registration).
5.  **Bank Onboarding**: Open a corporate account with a digital-ready bank (e.g., **Hashbank** or **TBC**).
6.  **Stablecoin Licensing**: Submit formal application to the **NBG** for stablecoin issuance (Post-bank account opening).
