*   **Fractionalization**: One asset, multiple owners/DAOs via digital tokens.
*   **Yield**: Rental income portions + property appreciation + RWA liquidity siphoning.
*   **Status**: Fractional ownership is the physical reification of RWA tokenization.

2.  **Fractional Silence**: Ownership is distributed; liability is fragmented.
